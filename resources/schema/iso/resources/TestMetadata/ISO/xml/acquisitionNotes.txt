Platforms, Instruments, and Sensors

The NASA ISO extensions include additions to the gmi:MI_Platform and gmi:MI_Instrument classes and introduce the new EOS:Sensor class. This set of tests explores what different namespace combinations are supported by the schema.

1. EOS_Platform with gmi:instrument/eos:EOS_Instrument
<xs:complexType name="EOS_Platform_Type">
